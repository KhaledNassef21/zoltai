### Top AI Background Removal Tools
#### Canva AI: All-in-One Editing Solution
**Canva AI** suits beginners needing a comprehensive editing platform. Its Background Remover delivers accurate results by detecting product outlines precisely.

**Features Include**:
- One-click background removal
- Extensive template library
- Marketing design tools
- Batch processing enabled

**Pricing**: Free with watermarks; Pro starts at $12.99/month

**Using Canva AI**:
1. Upload your photo to Canva.
2. Select "Edit Image," then "Background Remover."
3. AI eliminates the background.
4. Choose or upload custom templates for the background.
5. Fine-tune edges as needed.
6. Download in high resolution.

#### Remove.bg: Precision Background Removal
**Remove.bg** offers unmatched precision, streamlining workflows. Ideal for complex edges like hair.

**Key Features**:
- Excellent for complex edges
- API for automation
- Bulk processing
- High-resolution outputs

**Pricing**: Free for low-resolution; HD plans from $9/month

### Advanced Image Enhancement Tools

**Leonardo AI** specializes in generating lifestyle shots and enhancements.

**Use Cases**:

**Pricing**: Free tier with daily credits; paid plans start at $10/month

**Enhancement Steps**:

4. Adjust strength (30-50%).
