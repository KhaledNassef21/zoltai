## Best AI Video Editing Software in 2023

Elevate your video editing with leading AI software. Whether you're starting out or have editing experience, tools like **Canva AI**, **Descript**, and **Runway** can boost your productivity!

### Canva AI

**Canva AI** makes video editing easy with its AI-powered tools. Key features include:

1. **Drag and Drop Interface**: User-friendly for beginners to create videos, including transitions and text overlays.
2. **Template Selection**: Access numerous templates for quick, professional edits.
3. **Instant Animation**: Enhance engagement with lively animations, perfect for social media.

💡 *Pricing*: Free tier available with premium features, ideal for beginners.

### Descript

**Descript** is tailored for podcasters and vloggers, combining editing with AI transcription. Features include:

1. **AI Transcription and Overdub**: Convert voice to text and simplify both video and audio editing.
2. **Collaborative Tools**: Supports team editing for seamless video production.
3. **Screen Recording**: Integrated feature for creating tutorials efficiently.

💡 *Pricing*: Free version available, with extensive paid plans, favored by podcasters.

### Runway

**Runway** provides powerful AI features for video, image, and audio editing. Highlights include:

1. **Real-time Multi-user Collaboration**: Enables smooth teamwork on projects.
2. **Magic Motion Automation**: Automates tasks like object tracking and background removal.
3. **Style Transfer**: Create unique visuals with innovative style applications.

💡 *Pricing*: Offers a free trial and scalable paid tiers. Suitable for advanced editing needs.
