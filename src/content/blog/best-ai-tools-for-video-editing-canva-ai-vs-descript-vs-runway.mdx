## Best AI Video Editing Software in 2023

Upgrade your video editing with the top AI software. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned editor, tools like **Canva AI**, **Descript**, and **Runway** elevate productivity and creativity.

### Canva AI

**Canva AI** offers user-friendly video editing powered by AI. Its standout features include:

1. **Drag and Drop Interface**: Easy for beginners, facilitates video creation with transitions and text overlays.
2. **Template Selection**: Access numerous templates for swift, professional edits.
3. **Instant Animation**: Boost engagement with dynamic animations, ideal for social media.

💡 *Pricing*: Free tier with premium features, great for novices exploring video editing.

### Descript

**Descript** caters to podcasters and vloggers, merging video editing with AI transcription. Key features:

1. **AI Transcription and Overdub**: Convert voice to text, easing video and audio editing.
2. **Collaborative Tools**: Supports team editing for seamless production.
3. **Screen Recording**: Built-in for efficient tutorial creation.

💡 *Pricing*: Free version with detailed paid plans, popular with podcasters and vloggers.

### Runway

**Runway** boasts powerful AI for video, image, and audio editing. Notable features:

1. **Real-time Multi-user Collaboration**: Ensures smooth teamwork on projects.
2. **Magic Motion Automation**: Automates object tracking and background removal.
3. **Style Transfer**: Create unique visuals with innovative styles.

💡 *Pricing*: Free trial offered with scalable paid options, ideal for those needing advanced tools.
