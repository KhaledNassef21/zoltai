## Top AI Video Editing Software 2023

Enhance your video editing skills with leading AI video editors. Whether starting out or a pro, tools like **Canva AI Video Editor**, **Descript AI Video Tools**, and **Runway AI Software** boost productivity and creativity effectively.

### Canva AI Video Editor

**Canva AI Video Editor** features user-friendly tools powered by AI. Major features include:

1. **Drag and Drop Interface**: Perfect for beginners, simplify video creation with transitions and text overlays. 
2. **Template Selection**: Access various templates for quick, professional edits. 
3. **Instant Animation**: Enhance engagement with dynamic animations, great for social media.

💡 *Pricing*: Offers a free tier with premium features, ideal for novices exploring video editing.

### Descript AI Video Tools

**Descript AI Video Tools** cater to podcasters, merging editing with AI transcription. Key tools include:

1. **AI Transcription and Overdub**: Convert voice to text, simplifying video and audio editing.
2. **Collaborative Tools**: Supports team editing for streamlined production.
3. **Screen Recording**: Built-in tool for easy tutorial creation.

💡 *Pricing*: Free version with robust paid plans, favored by podcasters and vloggers.

### Runway AI Software

**Runway AI Software** offers powerful tools for video, image, and audio editing. Highlighted features:

1. **Real-time Multi-user Collaboration**: Facilitates smooth team projects.
2. **Magic Motion Automation**: Automates object tracking and background removal.
3. **Style Transfer**: Create unique visuals with innovative styles.

💡 *Pricing*: Free trial available with advanced paid options, perfect for those requiring comprehensive tools.
