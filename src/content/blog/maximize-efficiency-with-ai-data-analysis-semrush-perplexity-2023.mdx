Semrush delivers comprehensive digital marketing solutions focused on SEO, market research, and content marketing. Features include powerful keyword research, thorough site audits for SEO, and in-depth competitor strategy analysis.

1. **Keyword Research**: Utilize Semrush's Keyword Magic Tool to discover effective keywords.
2. **Site Audits**: Conduct detailed site audits to identify and address SEO issues.
