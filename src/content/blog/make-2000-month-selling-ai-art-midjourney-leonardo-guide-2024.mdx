### Step 1: Choose Your Niche (Day 1)

Successful AI art sellers focus on specific niches. Here are the most profitable:

**High-Demand Niches:**
- **Abstract wall art** ($15-50 per print)
- **Motivational quotes with backgrounds** ($5-25 per design)
- **Pet portraits** ($20-100 per custom piece)
- **Vintage travel posters** ($10-40 per print)
- **Minimalist home decor** ($8-30 per design)

**Pro Tip:** Start with abstract art—it's forgiving for beginners and has broad appeal.

### Step 2: Master Your Prompts (Days 2-5)

Great prompts = great art = great sales. Here's my proven formula:

**Winning Prompt Structure:**
`[Subject] + [Style] + [Mood/Color] + [Technical specs] + [Exclusions]`

**Example Profitable Prompts:**

```
Abstract flowing waves, minimalist geometric style, warm golden and deep blue colors, clean composition, high contrast --ar 3:4 --no text, people

Vintage travel poster of mountain landscape, art deco style, sunset colors, bold typography area, professional poster design --ar 2:3 --no modern elements

Motivational workspace flat lay, modern photography style, bright natural lighting, clean aesthetic, space for text overlay --ar 16:9 --no clutter
```
