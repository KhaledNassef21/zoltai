**Popular AI Art Niches:**

- **Custom Pet Portraits** ($20-100 each)

**Pro Tip:** Abstract art is a broad and accessible niche, making it perfect for beginners.

### Step 2: Compose Winning AI Art Prompts

Craft well-structured prompts to ensure sales:

**Effective AI Art Prompt Structure:**
`[Main Subject] + [Art Style] + [Emotional Tone/Color Scheme] + [Technical Specs] + [Exclusions]`

**Example Prompts:**
```
Abstract waves, minimalist, warm gold and deep blue, clean layout, high contrast --ar 3:4 --no text, no humans

Vintage poster of mountain landscape, art deco, sunset hues, bold typography spot, professional design --ar 2:3 --no modern features

Motivational desk flat lay, modern style, bright light, clean design, text space --ar 16:9 --no clutter
```

### Step 3: Create Your Initial AI Art Collection

**Generate AI Art with Midjourney:**
