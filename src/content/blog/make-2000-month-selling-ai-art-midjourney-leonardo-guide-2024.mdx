### Step 2: Craft Effective AI Art Prompts

Structure prompts to boost sales potential:

`[Main Subject] + [Style] + [Tone/Color] + [Specs] + [Exclusions]`

**Example Prompts:**
```
Abstract waves, minimalist, warm gold and deep blue, clean layout, high contrast --ar 3:4 --no text, no humans

Vintage poster of mountain landscape, art deco, sunset hues, bold typography spot, professional design --ar 2:3 --no modern features

Motivational desk flat lay, modern style, bright light, clean design, text space --ar 16:9 --no clutter
```
