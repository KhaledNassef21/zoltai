## Practical Applications of AI Tools

### Canva AI for Visual Design

**Canva AI** is perfect for creating professional visuals. It offers customizable templates with AI-driven layout, color, and font recommendations.

#### Step-by-Step: Creating a Header with Canva

1. Log into Canva, select 'Website Header' template.
2. Use AI assistant for style selection matching your brand.
3. Customize header with logo and text.
4. Download and integrate into your site.

### Midjourney for Artistic Design

**Midjourney** creates unique, AI-generated art for hero images or backgrounds.

#### Use Case: Generating a Hero Image

1. Access Midjourney, enter prompts related to your brand.
2. Choose from AI-designed options.
3. Refine with Midjourney’s tools.
4. Save and upload.

### Content Creation with ChatGPT and Jasper

**ChatGPT** and **Jasper** generate original web content.

#### Example: Writing an About Page

1. Input company details into ChatGPT.
2. Select tone and style.
3. Edit with Jasper for consistency and SEO.

### Automate with Zapier

Automate tasks like form data to customer databases with **Zapier**, streamlining workflow.

#### Automating Form Submissions

1. Set up form with hosting provider.
2. Use Zapier for automatic logging into Google Sheets.
3. Analyze data with **Perplexity** for insights.

## Which Tool Should You Start With?

For beginners, **Canva AI** is an ideal start due to its intuitive, user-friendly interface. It's free, with advanced feature options in paid plans. Explore **Midjourney** for creative designs and **Zapier** for automation as you grow comfortable.
