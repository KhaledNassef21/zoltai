## Top AI Tools for Viral TikToks

### Video Creation and Editing

1. **Runway AI**
   - **Why Use Runway?** Easily create and edit videos with AI. Includes background removal, effect addition, and animation features.
   - **Pricing**: Free tier with advanced features in paid plans.
   - **How to Use**:
     1. Import videos to Runway.
     2. Edit using AI Magic Tools, like 'Backdrop' for background changes.
     3. Export and share directly to TikTok.

2. **Canva AI**
   - **Why Use Canva AI?** Great for creating video graphics and visual effects.
   - **Pricing**: Free with options for premium features.
   - **How to Use**:
     1. Select a TikTok video template.
     2. Add effects and animations with AI tools.
     3. Customize and download.

### Content and Caption Generation

3. **ChatGPT or Claude**
   - **Why Use This?** For catchy video captions and unique content ideas.
   - **Pricing**: Free level; premium offers detailed responses.
   - **How to Use**:
     1. Enter your TikTok's theme.
     2. Request captions or scripts.
     3. Refine to suit your style.

4. **Jasper AI**
   - **Why Use Jasper?** Ideal for TikTok descriptions and hooks.
   - **Pricing**: Try for free; paid plans offer more features.
   - **How to Use**:
     1. Input your keywords or themes.
     2. Use AI-generated captions.

### Audio Enhancement

5. **ElevenLabs**
   - **Why Use ElevenLabs?** Adds charm to videos with realistic voiceovers.
   - **Pricing**: Free and premium plans available.
   - **How to Use**:
     1. Select or customize a voice.
     2. Add script and render audio.
     3. Sync audio to your video.

## Getting Started

For beginners, **Runway** is the best entry point due to its intuitive video editing capabilities. It ensures smooth, creative enhancements to your TikToks using AI tools.
