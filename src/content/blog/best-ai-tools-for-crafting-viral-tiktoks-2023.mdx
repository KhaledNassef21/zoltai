## Top AI Tools for Viral TikToks

### Video Creation and Editing

1. **Runway**
   - **Why Use Runway?** Effortlessly create and edit videos with innovative AI features. The tool is excellent for removing backgrounds, adding effects, and creating animations.
   - **Pricing**: Offers a free tier with paid plans for advanced features.
   - **Steps to Use**:
     1. Import your video clips to Runway.
     2. Use AI Magic Tools to edit. Try the 'Backdrop' feature to change backgrounds.
     3. Export and share directly to TikTok.

2. **Canva AI**
   - **Why Use Canva AI?** Perfect for creating eye-catching video graphics and incorporating visual effects effortlessly.
   - **Pricing**: Free with premium features available.
   - **Steps to Use**:
     1. Choose a TikTok video template.
     2. Use AI tools to add effects and animations.
     3. Customize and download.

### Content and Caption Generation

3. **ChatGPT or Claude**
   - **Why Use ChatGPT?** For generating catchy video captions and unique content ideas.
   - **Pricing**: ChatGPT offers a free tier; premium offers more detailed responses.
   - **Steps to Use**:
     1. Input the theme or subject of your TikTok.
     2. Request catchy captions or scripts.
     3. Adjust and refine to match your style.

4. **Jasper**
   - **Why Use Jasper?** Expert in creating engaging, concise copy perfect for TikTok descriptions and hooks.
   - **Pricing**: Free trial available, with paid plans for additional features.
   - **Steps to Use**:
     1. Enter your keywords or themes into Jasper.
     2. Use the AI-generated options as your caption.

### Audio Enhancement

5. **ElevenLabs**
   - **Why Use ElevenLabs?** Creates realistic voiceovers that can add unique charm to your videos.
   - **Pricing**: Free plan with premium options available.
   - **Steps to Use**:
     1. Choose a voice profile or customize your own.
     2. Add your script and render the audio.
     3. Sync the audio to your video.

## Which Tool Should You Start With?

If you’re new to AI tools, **Runway** is an excellent starting point for its intuitive video editing features. It's simple to use and provides immediate enhancements to your TikTok content, making your first steps into AI-powered creativity smooth and enjoyable.
