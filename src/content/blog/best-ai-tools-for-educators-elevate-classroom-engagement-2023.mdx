## Enhance Classroom Engagement with AI Tools

AI tools are transforming education, providing teachers with powerful methods to engage students. From interactive quizzes to personalized presentations, these tools help create enriched learning environments.

### Engage Students with AI Technology

Integrating AI tools enhances educational experiences beyond the introduction of new technology. Teachers can develop captivating lessons, facilitate personalized learning, and automate tasks to focus more on teaching.

### Best AI Tools for Teachers

1. **ChatGPT and Claude for Interactive Q&A**
   - **Use Case**: Facilitate engaging Q&A sessions.
   - **How To Use**: Utilize ChatGPT for real-time classroom queries. Claude generates discussion topics from the curriculum.
   - **Recommendation**: Start with ChatGPT for its free options.

2. **Canva AI for Visual Content Creation**
   - **Use Case**: Design educational posters, presentations, and more.
   - **How To Use**: Use Canva AI templates for engaging visual content.
   - **Recommendation**: Canva AI is intuitive and offers free usage.

3. **Perplexity AI for Personalized Learning Paths**
   - **Use Case**: Create personalized learning materials.
   - **How To Use**: Input student data to Perplexity AI for custom learning resources.
   - **Recommendation**: Use Perplexity AI for tailored education.

### Daily AI Classroom Integration Example

1. **Setup ChatGPT for Interactive Q&A**
   - Register at OpenAI.
   - Use ChatGPT in classroom forums.
   - Facilitate real-time Q&A.

2. **Craft Lessons with Canva AI**
   - Access Canva AI and choose a template.
   - Customize for weekly lessons.
   - Share via the school's LMS.

3. **Use Perplexity AI for Personalized Learning**
   - Upload anonymized student information.
   - Create custom resources for study sessions.
