## Boost Classroom Engagement with Top AI Tools

AI tools are revolutionizing education, giving teachers powerful ways to foster student engagement. From lively quizzes to tailored presentations, these tools enrich learning environments.

### Engage Students with AI Technology

Incorporating AI tools goes beyond just introducing new tech; it upgrades educational experiences. Teachers can craft captivating lessons, enable personalized learning, and automate mundane tasks, freeing more time for effective teaching.

### Best AI Tools for Teachers

1. **ChatGPT and Claude for Interactive Q&A**
   - **Use Case**: Conduct engaging Q&A sessions.
   - **How To Use**: Employ ChatGPT for real-time class queries. Claude generates curriculum-based discussion topics.
   - **Recommendation**: Begin with ChatGPT for its cost-free options.

2. **Canva AI for Visual Content Creation**
   - **Use Case**: Design educational posters, presentations, and more.
   - **How To Use**: Use Canva AI templates for dynamic visual content.
   - **Recommendation**: Canva AI is user-friendly with free use available.

3. **Perplexity AI for Personalized Learning Paths**
   - **Use Case**: Develop personalized learning materials.
   - **How To Use**: Input student data into Perplexity AI for custom learning resources.
   - **Recommendation**: Utilize Perplexity AI for tailored education.

### Daily AI Classroom Integration Example

1. **Setup ChatGPT for Interactive Q&A**
   - Sign up at OpenAI.
   - Use ChatGPT in class forums.
   - Enable real-time Q&A.

2. **Design Lessons with Canva AI**
   - Log into Canva AI and pick a template.
   - Customize for weekly lessons.
   - Share through the school's LMS.

3. **Use Perplexity AI for Personalized Learning**
   - Upload anonymized student data.
   - Generate custom resources for study sessions.
