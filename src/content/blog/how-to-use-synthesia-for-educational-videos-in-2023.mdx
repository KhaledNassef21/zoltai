## How to Get Started with Synthesia AI

### Step 1: Sign Up and Choose a Plan

Visit **Synthesia AI**'s website, register for an account, and select a plan. Choose from a free tier or paid plans for advanced features.

### Step 2: Select a Video Template

Browse **Synthesia AI**'s templates aligned with your educational objectives. Templates are categorized by industry and educational purpose.

### Step 3: Customize Your AI Avatar

Design customizable AI avatars with attributes that match your content's tone. Avatars add a human element to your video, enhancing relatability.

### Step 4: Script Your Educational Content

Compose scripts directly in **Synthesia AI**. Use **ChatGPT** or **Copy.ai** for script enhancement and refinement.

### Step 5: Integrate AI Voice

Enhance your educational video with AI-generated voiceovers. **ElevenLabs** provides realistic audio that integrates seamlessly with your avatar.

### Step 6: Preview and Export Your Video

Preview the educational video to ensure all elements align. Once satisfied, export your educational video in the desired format.
