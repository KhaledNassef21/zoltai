## How to Get Started with Synthesia

### Step 1: Sign Up and Choose a Plan

Visit **Synthesia**'s website and register for an account. Choose from a free tier or paid plans to access advanced features and customization.

### Step 2: Select a Video Template

Browse **Synthesia**'s array of templates to find one that aligns with your educational content objectives. Templates are organized by industry and purpose.

### Step 3: Customize Your Avatar

Select or design customizable AI avatars with physical attributes and attire matching your educational content's tone. Avatars serve as spokespersons, adding a human touch to your video.

### Step 4: Script Your Content

Compose scripts directly within **Synthesia**. Leverage tools like **ChatGPT** or **Copy.ai** for inspiration and script enhancement.

### Step 5: Integrate AI Voice

Augment your video with AI-generated voiceovers. **ElevenLabs** is recommended for producing realistic, high-quality audio that fits seamlessly with your Synthesia avatar.

### Step 6: Preview and Export

Review your video to ensure all elements align perfectly. Once content, export your educational video in the desired format.
