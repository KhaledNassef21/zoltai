## How to Get Started with Synthesia

### Step 1: Sign Up and Choose a Plan

Visit **Synthesia**'s website and sign up for an account. Choose from a free tier or paid plans to unlock advanced features and customization.

### Step 2: Select a Video Template

Explore **Synthesia**'s variety of templates. Choose a template that best suits your educational content goals. Templates are categorized by industry and purpose.

### Step 3: Customize Your Avatar

Choose from customizable AI avatars or create your own. Select physical attributes and clothing to match your educational content's tone. The avatar acts as the spokesperson, adding a human touch to your video.

### Step 4: Script Your Content

Script directly within **Synthesia**. Use tools like **ChatGPT** or **Copy.ai** for inspiration or refinement of your script.

### Step 5: Integrate AI Voice

Enhance your video with AI-generated voiceovers. **ElevenLabs** is recommended for creating realistic, high-quality audio that synchronizes smoothly with your Synthesia avatar.

### Step 6: Preview and Export

Preview your video to ensure all elements align perfectly. Once satisfied, export your video in the desired format.
