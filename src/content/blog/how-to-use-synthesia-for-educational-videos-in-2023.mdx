## How to Get Started with Synthesia

### Step 1: Sign Up and Choose a Plan

First, visit **Synthesia**'s website and sign up for an account. Choose from a free tier or paid plans to unlock advanced features and customization options.

### Step 2: Select a Video Template

Explore the variety of templates **Synthesia** offers. Select a template that suits your educational content best. Templates are categorized by industry and purpose, ensuring you find the right fit for your goals.

### Step 3: Customize Your Avatar

Choose from customizable AI avatars or create your own. This step involves selecting physical attributes and clothing to match the tone of your educational content. The avatar acts as the spokesperson, adding a human touch to your video.

### Step 4: Script Your Content

Script your video directly within **Synthesia**. For inspiration or refinement, leverage tools like **ChatGPT** or **Copy.ai** to brainstorm ideas and polish your script.

### Step 5: Integrate AI Voice

Enhance your video with AI-generated voiceovers. **ElevenLabs** is recommended for creating realistic and high-quality audio that synchronizes smoothly with your Synthesia avatar.

### Step 6: Preview and Export

Preview your video to ensure all elements align perfectly. Once satisfied, export your video in the desired format.
