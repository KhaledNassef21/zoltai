## Designing with Canva AI
Visuals dominate social media and Canva AI makes creating eye-catching designs simple.

### Easy Design Creation
- **AI Design Suggestions:** Select a style and let Canva AI provide design ideas that align with your brand.
- **Magic Resize:** Create a design once and resize it for different platforms instantly.

### Canva AI Best Practices
- Begin with a template or a blank slate.
- Utilize the "Brand Kit" for consistent visuals.
- Export designs easily to social media channels.

Canva AI offers user-friendly features for beginners and robust tools for design experts, making the most of AI insights.
