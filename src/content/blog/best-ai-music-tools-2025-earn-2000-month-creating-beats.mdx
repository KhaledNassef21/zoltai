## Common Pitfalls in AI Music Creation

1. **Unappealing Track Names:** Names like "AI Beat 001" are less appealing. Opt for "Dark Trap Beat 2025 - Underground Vibes"
2. **Overlooking Copyright:** Always verify commercial rights prior to sales
3. **Neglecting Quality Control:** Showcase only top AI-generated work
4. **Inconsistent Updates:** Success requires a steady flow of creation and uploads
5. **Competitive Underpricing:** Focus on adding value over competing purely on price

## Legal Aspects of AI Music

**Ensure commercial rights** for any music you plan to sell. Various platforms offer distinct licensing tiers:
- **Personal Use:** Often free
- **Commercial Use:** Paid subscriptions necessary
- **Exclusive Rights:** Available in premium pricing plans

**Maintain thorough documentation** of your creations and agreements for legal and tax protection.
