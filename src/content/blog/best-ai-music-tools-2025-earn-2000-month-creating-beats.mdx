## Common Mistakes to Avoid with AI Music Creation

1. **Generic AI Track Names**: "AI Beat 001" won't sell. Use descriptive titles like "Dark Trap Beat 2025 - AI Generated Underground Vibes"

2. **Ignoring AI Music Copyright**: Always ensure commercial rights for your AI-generated music sales

3. **Poor Quality Control**: Not all AI music outputs are sellable. Curate only your best AI-generated work

4. **Inconsistent AI Music Uploading**: Success requires regular AI music creation and uploads

5. **Underpricing AI Music**: Don't compete on price alone with AI-generated beats. Build value through quality

## Legal Considerations for AI Music Creators

**Always verify commercial rights** for AI-generated music you plan to sell. Most AI music platforms offer different licensing tiers:
- **Personal use**: Usually free for AI music generators
- **Commercial use**: Requires paid subscription for AI music tools
- **Exclusive rights**: Premium pricing tiers for AI-generated music

**Keep detailed records** of your AI music creative process and licensing agreements. This protects you legally and helps with tax preparation if you sell your work.
