## Common Mistakes That Kill Your Income

1. **Generic Track Names**: "Beat 001" won't sell. Use descriptive, searchable titles like "Dark Trap Beat 2025 - Underground Vibes"

2. **Ignoring Copyright**: Always ensure you have commercial rights to sell your AI-generated music

3. **Poor Quality Control**: Not all AI outputs are sellable. Curate your best work only

4. **Inconsistent Uploading**: Success requires regular content creation and uploads

5. **Underpricing**: Don't compete on price alone. Build value through quality and service

## Legal Considerations and Best Practices

**Always verify commercial rights** for any AI-generated music you plan to sell. Most platforms offer different licensing tiers:
- **Personal use**: Usually free
- **Commercial use**: Requires paid subscription
- **Exclusive rights**: Premium pricing tiers

**Keep detailed records** of your creative process and licensing agreements. This protects you legally and helps with tax preparation as your income grows.
