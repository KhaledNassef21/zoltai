## Common AI Music Creation Mistakes

1. **Generic Track Names:** "AI Beat 001" won't sell. Use "Dark Trap Beat 2025 - Underground Vibes"
2. **Ignoring Copyright:** Always ensure commercial rights for sales
3. **Poor Quality Control:** Curate only your best AI-generated work
4. **Inconsistent Uploading:** Success requires regular creation and uploads
5. **Underpricing:** Don't compete on price alone - build value through quality

## Legal Considerations for AI Music

**Always verify commercial rights** for AI-generated music you plan to sell. Most platforms offer different licensing tiers:
- **Personal use:** Usually free
- **Commercial use:** Requires paid subscription
- **Exclusive rights:** Premium pricing tiers

**Keep detailed records** of your creative process and licensing agreements for legal protection and tax purposes.
