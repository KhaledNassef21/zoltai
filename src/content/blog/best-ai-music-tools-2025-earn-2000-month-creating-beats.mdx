## Monetizing AI Music Generators

With a rapidly growing AI-created music market, numerous monetization strategies exist:

**Freelance Custom AI Music**
- Background tunes for YouTube creators.  
- Intros and outros for podcasters.  
- Unique audio content for social media managers.

**Music Licensing Platforms**
- Publish on sites like [Pond5](https://www.pond5.com), [AudioJungle](https://audiojungle.net), [Shutterstock Music](https://www.shutterstock.com/music).  
- Earn royalties from downloads and streams.  
- Develop catalogs to generate passive income.

**Beat Selling Platforms**
- Sell on [BeatStars](https://www.beatstars.com) and [Airbit](https://airbit.com) for AI-generated beats.  
- Offer non-exclusive and exclusive licenses.  
- Build a reputation through consistent uploads.

**Partnerships with Content Creators**
- Supply regular music to YouTube channels.  
- Collaborate with brands using AI music tools.  
- Offer custom AI compositions for video series.
