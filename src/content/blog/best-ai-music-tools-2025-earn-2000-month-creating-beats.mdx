## Legal Considerations for AI Music Business

**Always verify commercial rights** for AI-generated music you plan to sell. Most AI music platforms offer different licensing tiers:
- **Personal use**: Usually free for AI music generators
- **Commercial use**: Requires paid subscription for AI music tools
- **Exclusive rights**: Premium pricing tiers for AI-generated music

**Keep detailed records** of your AI music creative process and licensing agreements. This protects your AI music business legally and helps with tax preparation.
