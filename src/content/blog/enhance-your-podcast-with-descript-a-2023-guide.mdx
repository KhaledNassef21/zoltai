## Getting Started with Descript

### Step 1: Install Descript

To get started with Descript, visit their official website and download the app. Descript offers a free tier to experience its core features, with paid plans available for advanced functionalities like more transcription hours and watermark removal.

### Step 2: Import Your Audio

Once installed, import your podcast audio files. Descript supports various audio formats, making it easy to integrate with your existing podcast content and start editing.

### Step 3: Transcription

Descript automatically transcribes your podcast audio, transforming episodes into text documents—valuable for quickly spotting dialogue weaknesses and editing more efficiently.

### Step 4: Edit Like a Pro

Descript lets you edit your podcast audio like a document. Highlight text to cut sections from your audio—simplifying podcast editing and saving you time.

### Step 5: Enhance Audio Quality

Improve your podcast's quality using Descript’s Studio Sound feature. This AI tool cleans and sharpens your audio, reducing background noise.

### Step 6: Export and Publish

Once satisfied with your edits, easily export podcast files in your preferred format and upload them to your preferred podcast hosting service.
