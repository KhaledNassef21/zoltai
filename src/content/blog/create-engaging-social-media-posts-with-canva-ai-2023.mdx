### Step-by-Step Guide to Using Canva AI

#### Step 1: Sign Up

- **Visit** [Canva](https://www.canva.com) and register for a free account.
- **Upgrade** to access more features anytime.

#### Step 2: Select a Template

- **Browse Social Media**: Choose a template for platforms like Instagram, Facebook, or Twitter.
- **Select Template**: Pick a design that suits your theme.

#### Step 3: Customize Designs

- **Text Suggestions**: Let Canva AI auto-generate relevant text with 'Magic Write'.
