## Launching Your AI Voice Business

### Step 1: Identify Your Niche (Week 1)

Focus on a profitable niche, not everyone:

**High-Demand Niches:**  
- YouTube explainer videos  
- E-learning courses  
- Audiobook narration  
- Meditation content  
- Product demos

### Step 2: Set Up AI Tools (Day 1-2)

1. **Sign Up for Murf AI** (Start free)  
2. **Create Niche Samples**  
3. **Build Profiles** on Fiverr, Upwork, Freelancer  
4. **Develop Portfolio** With sample work

### Step 3: Create AI Voice Samples (Week 1)

**Example - YouTube Explainer:**

1. **Script:** Use ChatGPT for 2-minute script.  
2. **Voice Selection:** Choose a professional AI voice.  
3. **Enhance Delivery:** Use pauses and emphasis.  
4. **Export:** Download as MP3.  
5. **Time:** 15-minute investment.  
6. **Outcome:** High-quality portfolio sample.

**Tip:** Create 5-10 samples across niches for client appeal.

### Step 4: Strategic Pricing (Week 1)

**Pricing Strategy:**  
- Research competitive rates in your niche.  
- Start below market rates for reviews.  
- Highlight fast delivery.  
- Increase rates as your profile grows.

### Step 5: Acquire Your First Clients (Week 2-3)

**Platform Strategy:**

**Fiverr:**  
- Create multiple gigs with keywords: "AI voice," "professional voiceover."  
- Use fast delivery as a selling point.  
- Start with introductory pricing.

**Upwork:**  
- Focus on quick turnaround in proposals.  
- Showcase AI benefits, "Quality in 24 hours."  
- Target bigger projects.

**Direct Outreach:**  
- Connect with YouTube creators.  
- Reach out to course creators.  
- Engage with podcasters.

### Step 6: Deliver Quality Voiceovers (Ongoing)

**Quality Checklist:**  
- ✅ Review AI audio pre-delivery.  
- ✅ Offer 1-2 revision rounds free.  
- ✅ Deliver in multiple formats.  
- ✅ Add music when suitable.  
- ✅ Provide transcripts.
