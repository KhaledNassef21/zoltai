## Step-by-Step Guide to Launching Your AI Voice Business

### Step 1: Identify Your AI Voiceover Niche (Week 1)

Select a profitable niche instead of trying to serve everyone:

**High-Demand Niches:**
- YouTube explainer videos
- E-learning courses
- Audiobook narration
- Meditation/wellness content
- Product demos

### Step 2: Set Up AI Tools (Day 1-2)

1. **Sign Up for Murf AI** (Start free)
2. **Create Niche Samples**
3. **Build Profiles** on Fiverr, Upwork, Freelancer
4. **Develop Portfolio** With sample work

### Step 3: Create AI Voice Samples (Week 1)

**Example - YouTube Explainer:**

1. **Script:** Use ChatGPT to create a 2-minute script.
2. **Voice Selection:** Choose a professional AI voice.
3. **Enhance Delivery:** Use pauses and emphasis.
4. **Export:** Download polished MP3.
5. **Time:** 15-minute investment.
6. **Outcome:** High-quality portfolio sample.

**Tip:** Create 5-10 samples across niches for client appeal.

### Step 4: Strategic Pricing (Week 1)

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Research competitive rates in your niche.
- Start below market rate to gain reviews.
- Capitalize on fast delivery as an advantage.
- Gradually increase as your profile strengthens.

### Step 5: Acquire Your First Clients (Week 2-3)

**Platform Strategy:**

**Fiverr:**
- Create multiple AI voiceover gigs.
- Include keywords: "AI voice," "professional voiceover."
- Fast delivery as a selling point.
- Start with introductory pricing.

**Upwork:**
- Proposals should emphasize quick turnaround.
- Showcase AI benefit: "Quality in 24 hours."
- Target higher-budget projects.

**Direct Outreach:**
- Connect with YouTube creators.
- Approach course creators.
- Reach out to podcasters.

### Step 6: Deliver Quality Voiceovers (Ongoing)

**Quality Checklist:**
- ✅ Review full AI audio before delivery.
- ✅ Include 1-2 revision rounds gratis.
- ✅ Deliver in multiple formats.
- ✅ Add subtle music when suitable.
- ✅ Provide transcripts.
