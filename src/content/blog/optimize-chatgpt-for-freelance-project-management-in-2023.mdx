**Use Case**: Automate thank-you emails and status updates.

1. **Setup**: Use ChatGPT for email drafts.
2. **Execution**: Save templates for client greetings and project milestones.
3. **Optimization**: Regularly refresh templates for relevance.

### Task Organization

**Use Case**: Manage daily tasks with AI.

1. **Identifying Tasks**: List weekly tasks.
2. **Sorting and Scheduling**: Use ChatGPT to prioritize by deadline.
3. **Updating**: Generate daily summaries with ChatGPT to track tasks.

**Use Case**: Use AI for project-specific research.

1. **Information Gathering**: Input queries into ChatGPT for detailed insights.
2. **Resource Compilation**: Let ChatGPT gather resources in one document for easy access.
